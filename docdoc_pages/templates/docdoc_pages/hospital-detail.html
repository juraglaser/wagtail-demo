Overview

Farrer Park Hospital is conveniently located at Connexion, Singapore’s first integrated healthcare and hospitality complex ideally situated on top of Farrer Park MRT station. The new 20-storey building houses a spa, a hotel, and the Owen Link and features lush greenery that provides patients with a tranquil healing environment.

Combining advanced technology and medical expertise, Farrer Park Hospital offers a wide range of specialist, diagnostic, and imaging services for the assessment, prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of various medical conditions. It is staffed by over 300 accredited specialists with a wealth of experience in a range of medical and surgical specialties including cardiology, cardiothoracic surgery, oncology, gastroenterology, urology, and vascular surgery, among others.

The hospital’s world-class facilities include a 24-hour walk-in emergency clinic, 23-bed intensive care units (ICU), 18 modern operating theatres, a radiation oncology and nuclear medicine clinic, and a clinical laboratory, among others.

Hospital philosophy

Farrer Park Hospital is a state-of-the-art private tertiary healthcare institution committed to delivering quality medical care. Founded in 2009, its mission is to offer a fresh approach to medical treatment based on comfort, fairness, and value.
