Overview

VIDASKIN Medical and Aesthetics Clinic is conveniently located at Ngee Ann City (Tower B) on Orchard Road in Singapore. It offers a comprehensive range of aesthetic treatments for the face, hair, skin, body, and hands using advanced technology, including modern laser devices, and modern facilities.

The clinic is the private practice of Dr. Vicki Leong, an Australia, United Kingdom, and Singapore-trained medical aesthetic practitioner. She graduated from the University of Melbourne and completed her Diploma of Family Medicine from the National University of Singapore. With a keen interest in aesthetic medicine, she also pursued her Postgraduate Diploma in Practical Dermatology from Cardiff University (United Kingdom).

Dr. Vicki is the appointed regional trainer for EnerJetâ„¢, a nonsurgical face lifting, skin regeneration, and acne scar repair procedure.
